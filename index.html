STEM & Communication Enrichment Program

Welcome to a 12-week journey of learning, creating, and presenting! Designed for ambitious Grade 4 students, this program combines hands-on STEM activities with essential public speaking and teamwork skills.

Program Highlights

  • Weekly hands-on STEM projects (circuits, solar power, sensors, etc.)
  • Development of public speaking, collaboration, and problem-solving skills
  • Modular kits that build each week
  • Capstone showcase presentation for parents
